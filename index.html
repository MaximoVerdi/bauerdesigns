Our Olympic Peninsula Custom Services

Elevate your coastal home with our premium window treatments, designed to complement the natural beauty of coastal living.

Blinds

Sleek, modern, coastal-inspired window treatments designed for both style and functionality.

Blinds

Sleek, modern, coastal-inspired window treatments designed for both style and functionality.

Our custom blinds are crafted with premium materials and cutting-edge technology to provide the perfect balance of light control and privacy. We offer a wide range of styles including horizontal blinds, vertical blinds, and motorized options. Each blind is measured and installed with precision to ensure a perfect fit for your windows. Our coastal-inspired designs complement beachfront homes and modern interiors alike, while providing excellent UV protection and energy efficiency.

Shutters

Timeless, durable elegance that adds character and value to your coastal home.

Shutters

Timeless, durable elegance that adds character and value to your coastal home.

Our premium shutters are built to last, offering classic beauty and superior functionality. Available in both interior and exterior options, our shutters are manufactured using high-quality materials that withstand coastal weather conditions. Choose from traditional plantation styles, modern designs, or custom solutions. Each shutter is carefully crafted to your specifications and comes with a lifetime warranty. Perfect for creating that quintessential coastal look while providing excellent insulation and light control.

Drapes

Luxurious, tailored fabric solutions, perfectly matched to your home's unique aesthetic.

Drapes

Luxurious, tailored fabric solutions, perfectly matched to your home's unique aesthetic.

Transform your space with our custom drapery collections. We offer an extensive selection of fabrics, from light and airy sheers to rich, room-darkening materials. Our expert designers will help you select the perfect patterns, colors, and textures to complement your interior design. Each drape is professionally measured and sewn to ensure proper fullness and length. We specialize in layered window treatments that combine functionality with sophisticated coastal style.

Repairs

Restore your window treatments flawlessly with our expert repair and maintenance services.

Repairs

Restore your window treatments flawlessly with our expert repair and maintenance services.

Our professional repair service extends the life of your window treatments. Our experienced technicians can fix a wide range of issues, from broken mechanisms to cord replacements and fabric repairs. We service all major brands and styles of window treatments. Regular maintenance packages are available to keep your window treatments operating smoothly. We also offer emergency repair services for urgent situations, ensuring your home's privacy and comfort are never compromised.
About Us

Building high-quality custom window treatments since 1976

Bauer Interior Design has served the greater Sequim area with a focus on quality, value and customer service since being established in 1976 by Ed and Linda Bauer.

Now owned by daughter Heidi Bauer, an interior designer with over 30 years experience, we continue to offer new blinds, shutters, shades, draperies, blind repairs and furnishings.

Stop by our showroom and meet our onsite father/daughter management team, Brian and Bri Oechsner.
